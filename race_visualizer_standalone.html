ℹ️ How to read the graph:
Each colored line represents a runner's cumulative time progression through the race. The X-axis shows each control point, and the Y-axis shows the time in seconds. Disqualified runners (marked as "Hylätty" or "Keskeytti") are excluded from the visualization. All forkings are combined, showing only controls that appear in all variants.
